About Me

My name is Annett Bordoley but I usually go by Anat. I am currently working for Boeing Comercial Airplanes in their Loads and Dynamics organization in Mulkiteo, Washington. My home page has more information about what I've worked on the last couple years. So how does a florida girl end up on the opposite corner of the continental US? Well it all started when I moved to Philadelphia to work on my engineering degree at the University of Pennsylvania. Two Boeing internships later I was hooked on the idea that I could apply my skills to create something the flies. At the end of my second internship I interviewed for a rotational program Boeing has called Engineering Career Foundation Program. The program allows new hires to rotate through six different jobs across all their business units and through the full airplane development cycle. This was the perfect opportunity for me since although I knew I wanted to work on airplanes, I had no idea what I actually wanted to do. I finally found my niche on my sixth rotation in Loads and Dynamics and have worked there ever since.

Outside of work I love to travel, scuba dive, paint, hike, and listen to audio books. I'm currently working towards an undergraduate degree in computer science at Oregon State, which takes up a good chunk of my time outside of work.
