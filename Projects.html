Vortex Generators

Vortex Generators are place on wings mainly to keep the flow attached during take off and landing. In cruise however, these vortex generators induce drag. I designed a Boeing patented technology to deploy and retract vortex generators based on ambient conditions (air speed, temperature). US patent: EP2998219A1
